System Crash

You delete the pointer without checking what it belonged to: 

delete pointerToSomethingIDontKnow;

A deafening crack echoes across the North Pole. The world freezes mid-frame like a corrupted video.

Santa stands stuck forever in mid-laugh:

“Ho ho h—”

Elves groan. “Yeah… that was definitely the wrong pointer,” one mutters. “We’ll reboot Santa tomorrow.”

Bad Ending – but hilarious.
