The Valgrind Elf

A small figure steps out of the shadows—a tiny elf wearing a coat made of printed code. Electric sparks dance in his beard.

“I am the Valgrind Elf,” he says proudly. “Guardian of memory leaks, destroyer of bad pointers… and I bring terrible news.”

“Santa’s old C++ system is leaking memory at an unstoppable rate. If we don’t find the source soon, Christmas will crash!”

Two leads appear on his glowing debugging map:

  • The Pointer Labyrinth — where broken references wander forever.
  • dangling_pointer.txt — a corrupted file blinking like a heartbeat.
